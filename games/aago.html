Ann Arbor Go - Programmer

Ann Arbor is a recreation of Pokemon Go my partner and I made with Unity's AR tools. Players must explore landmarks to learn more about Ann Arbor, plant trees to collect currency, and fight off squirrels.

To track the player's real time location, we used Mapbox to provide GPS coordinates to the player. For the interactive AR mode, we use ray casting to locate surfaces and place objects so they fit within the world. We also added a special landmark which triggers a special squirrel attack event where players must fight of four squirrels before they lose all their money.
